Turning info to bet.

Triggy is an innovative and fun way for players to schedule relavant betting notifications. Player adds what they are interested in getting notified about and Triggy takes care of watching the game. Reducing the steps to live bet and enabling players to bet on more matches simultaneously.

"Notify me if the odds is increases above 2.44"
"Notify me if the home team scores first"
"Notify me if the odds is 2.55 and the score is 0-1"

Never miss a moment.

Triggy is always live and listening to what's happening in the game. As soon as all the triggers become true, Triggy sends out a relavant betting notification attached with a pre-populated betslip. So operators can rest assure that their players never miss out on betting due to slow navigation.

Triggy Auto Place.

Triggy is all about increasing turnover of live betting by shortening the path from info to bet. Through Triggy Auto Place operators have an optional feature to let users live bet fully automatically.
