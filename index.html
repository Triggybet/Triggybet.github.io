Turning info into bets.

Triggy is an innovative and fun way for players to schedule relevant betting notifications. Players add what they are interested in getting notified about and Triggy takes care of watching the game. Reducing the steps to live bet and enabling players to bet on more matches simultaneously.

"Notify me of all important events in the match"
"Notify me if the odds increases above 2.44"
"Notify me if the home team scores first"

Relevant Next Bets.

Triggy sends relevant notifications to the players with ‘next bet’ betting suggestions. ‘Next bets’ are personalized selections of bets based on the event that just happened, player bet history and AI, keeping relevancy and conversion in focus.

Never miss a moment.

Triggy never sleeps. It always listens to what happens in the match. Through Triggy Auto Place, operators have an optional feature to let users live bet automatically. This allows players to live bet on matches they can’t follow and on several matches simultaneously.
